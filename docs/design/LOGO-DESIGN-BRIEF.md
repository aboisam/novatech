# 🎨 NovaTech Solutions — Logo Design Brief

> **Company:** NovaTech Solutions  
> **Industry:** IT Services & Software Solutions  
> **Tagline:** "Empowering Africa's Digital Future — One Solution at a Time"  
> **Brand Personality:** Modern, trustworthy, innovative, professional, forward-thinking

---

## 1. Logo Concept / Idea

The NovaTech Solutions logo should visually represent **technology, innovation, and forward momentum** while feeling approachable and trustworthy. The name "Nova" means "new" (Latin) and references a nova star — a sudden burst of brilliant energy — symbolizing the company's mission to ignite digital transformation across Africa.

The logo should communicate:
- **Technology & Digital Innovation** — clean, geometric, digital feel
- **Growth & Forward Motion** — upward or forward-pointing energy
- **Trust & Professionalism** — stable, balanced, corporate-ready
- **African Identity** — subtle modern pride, not cliché

---

## 2. Icon / Symbol Description

### Primary Symbol: Abstract "N" + Circuit/Star Mark

**Shape:** A stylized letter **"N"** formed with geometric, angular lines that subtly incorporate:
- **A rising arrow or upward trajectory** within the letter shape — representing growth, innovation, and forward momentum
- **A small starburst or spark element** at the top-right corner of the "N" — referencing the "Nova" (star) concept
- **Clean circuit-line aesthetic** — thin geometric connections that hint at technology and digital networks

**Style Details:**
- The "N" should feel **architectural and structural** — not handwritten or decorative
- Lines should be **uniform in weight** with sharp, precise angles
- The starburst/spark should be **subtle** — 3-4 small radiating lines or a 4-pointed star, not overpowering
- Overall silhouette should fit comfortably within a **square bounding box** for versatility

### Alternative Symbol Option:
If the "N" approach is too complex, an **abstract hexagon** (representing connectivity, tech nodes, and structure) with an integrated upward arrow or spark element also works well.

---

## 3. Logo Type

### Primary: **Icon + Wordmark** (Combination Mark)

```
[ N✦ Icon ]  NovaTech Solutions
```

- **Icon** on the left, **wordmark** on the right (horizontal lockup)
- Also provide a **stacked version** (icon on top, wordmark below) for square layouts
- The word "Nova" in **bold weight**, "Tech" in **regular or medium weight** — creating a visual break
- "Solutions" appears below or beside "NovaTech" in a **smaller, lighter weight**

### Typography for Wordmark:
- **"NovaTech"** — Space Grotesk Bold (700) — matches the heading font from the design system
- **"Solutions"** — Inter Medium (500) or Space Grotesk Medium (500) — lighter, smaller, secondary

## 5. Style Keywords

| ✅ Use These Styles | Description |
| ------------------- | ----------- |
| **Modern** | Contemporary, current, not dated |
| **Minimal** | Clean, uncluttered, no unnecessary elements |
| **Flat** | No gradients, 3D effects, or heavy shadows in the icon |
| **Geometric** | Built from precise shapes — angles, straight lines, clean curves |
| **Professional** | Corporate-appropriate, not playful or casual |
| **Scalable** | Works at 16px favicon AND on a billboard |

---

## 6. What to AVOID

| ❌ Do NOT Use | Why |
| ------------- | --- |
| Globe/Earth imagery | Overused cliché in tech logos |
| Gears or cog icons | Feels industrial, not software/digital |
| Generic lightbulb | Too generic for "innovation" |
| African map outline | Too literal, potentially exclusionary |
| Gradient fills in the icon | Won't reproduce well in all contexts |
| Thin/hairline fonts | Poor readability at small sizes |
| More than 3 colors | Brand palette discipline |
| Rounded/bubbly shapes | Doesn't match the sharp, professional brand tone |
| 3D or skeuomorphic effects | Dated, doesn't match flat modern aesthetic |
| Script or serif fonts | Clashes with the geometric sans-serif brand |
| Clipart or stock icons | Must feel custom and unique |

## 9. Logo Sizing Guide

| Context | Minimum Size | Recommended Size |
| ------- | ------------ | --------------- |
| Website navbar | 120px wide | 150–180px wide |
| Footer | 100px wide | 130–150px wide |
| Favicon | 16×16px | 32×32px (with 16×16 and 48×48 variants) |
| Social media profile | 400×400px | 500×500px (icon only, centered) |
| Business card | 1.5 inches wide | 2 inches wide |
| Email signature | 150px wide | 200px wide |

---

## 10. Clear Space Rules

Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "N" in the wordmark. No other text, graphics, or elements should enter this space.

```
         ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
         │                                     │
         │     ▄  NovaTech                     │
         │    ▀▀  Solutions                    │
         │                                     │
         └─────────────────────────────────────┘
              ↑                              ↑
          Clear space                    Clear space
         (= height of N)              (= height of N)
```
